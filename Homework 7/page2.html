The Missing Keys

It's the dreadful year of 2020. Its the end of June, and my lease is up

I wake up at 7:00am. Ive saved every bit of packing and cleaning for the day I must be out

My friend meets me at 8:00am to help move out. He has a truck and I need it to move my stuff

We clean and we clean, we get all of my things outside the house, things are looking good

Then... I go to find my keys to open my car and start packing. But I cant seem to find them

I look some more, still cant find them

I start unpacking my clothes, everything Ive just put away, tearing apart bags, searching in every pants pocket

Hours pass, I drive to my parents looking for a spare, no spare. I realize it's locked in my car

We try to break into my car but cant get the door open. It's getting late and im feeling desprate

Around 7:30pm my roomate gets home, my things are scattered around the living room, torn apart from looking for my keys

"Carter is there any chance you have seen my keys?" "You mean those Keys?"

Carter points to the key hanger in the living room, right next to the front door, there hang my keys

I never put my keys there...
